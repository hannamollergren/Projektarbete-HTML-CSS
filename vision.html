Vision

As we all know, there is a climate crisis. Leaders around the world works on solutions for less climate emissions. Have they succeeded? No! Icaros Space Sales has come up with a sustainable solution, we will restore the earth to the state of origin. Our vision is to complete this construction by year 2060.

Until then, all individuals on earth will be distributed to different places in space. ISS sells planets, moons, galaxies and stars. We offer free travel with our spacecrafts to your new home. All our spacecrafts are provided with special sustainable fuel, zero environmental emissions. Click here to visit our shop!
